Biography

I’m an algebraic topologist working at Kungliga Tekniska Högskolan (KTH) in Stockholm. Previously, I’ve worked in the same racket in Amsterdam and Münster; I got my PhD at MIT and studied in Oxford and Bonn.

I spend most of my days doing research, teaching, and organizing things at the department and outside.

At Stockholm, we’re collaborating with Stockholms universitet (SU) in all matters research and graduate teaching. Since June 2024, I am co-director of the joint (KTH/SU) Master’s program in math. Before, I have been director of the PhD program in math for seven years.
