The realizability of local loop spaces as manifolds

Abstract

As an extension of earlier work, we show that every $P$-local loop space, where $P$ is a set of primes, is homotopy equivalent to the $P$-localization of a compact, smooth, stably parallelizable manifold. A similar result is also proved for $P$-complete loop spaces.

Publication
$K$-theory 37/3 (2006), pp. 329–339
